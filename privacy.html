SuperSSH 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 13 日

SuperSSH 是一款 SSH 终端客户端。我们不收集、不存储、不传输你的任何个人数据。

数据存储

你的主机配置（地址、端口、用户名）保存在你设备本地的数据库中；密码与私钥保存在设备的系统钥匙串（Keychain）中，受系统加密与可选的生物识别（Face ID / Touch ID）保护。这些数据不会离开你的设备，也不会被发送给开发者或任何第三方。

网络连接

App 产生的全部网络流量仅限于你主动发起的 SSH 连接——即你的设备与你指定的服务器之间的加密通信。App 不包含分析、统计、广告或崩溃上报 SDK，不与开发者的服务器通信（开发者也没有服务器）。

第三方

无。App 不集成任何第三方服务。

SuperSSH Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

SuperSSH is an SSH terminal client. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

Data Storage

Your host configurations (address, port, username) are stored in a local database on your device. Passwords and private keys are stored in the system Keychain, protected by system encryption and optional biometrics (Face ID / Touch ID). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to the developer or any third party.

Network Connections

All network traffic generated by the app is limited to SSH connections you initiate — encrypted communication between your device and the servers you specify. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs, and does not communicate with any developer server (there is none).

Third Parties

None. The app integrates no third-party services.
